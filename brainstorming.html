Proposed Solution To Mitigate Typhoon Impacts

Implement and inform readiness before, during, and after a typhoon and how to mitigate the impact of the typhoon. In our suggested strategy, we will explain how to mitigate the typhoon's effects or decrease its damage. Now, our plan for reducing typhoon impact is to keep residences far from agricultural regions, build earthquake-resistant roadways, establish and uphold reasonable building regulations to safeguard assets from typhoons, and encourage people to plant more trees or make more organizations about nature and planting more trees as it can help the flooding from the typhoon. Furthermore, we can help with typhoon mitigation through awareness, training, preparation, forecasting, and alert systems. These preparations are potentially beneficial since people have the opportunity to prepare, purchase supplies, and secure their houses, medical equipment, and other crucial things.

Brainstorming Techniques

Mind Map

This Mind map is our first brainstorming tool that consists of the problem and solution when it comes to the Typhoon Glenda’s impact that is flooding. This solution can also contribute and be applied to other typhoons as well. The solution that this tool has is to lessen the impacts of flooding by planting vegetation. It is an activity that involves planting more trees and other plants that can help by absorbing more water into the soil that can also help for the prevention of soil erosion.

FishBone Diagram

This tool is what we call the fishbone diagram that helps us in having a case and effect process when it comes to flooding. We can see that our main cause of this is the improper disposal of garbage. Trash we toss anywhere clogs drains and eventually clogs drainages and esteros, impeding the free flow of water and exacerbating floods.

5 Whys

This tool is called the 5 whys and this aims to find the root cause of why the flooding is worsening. We can see that the main reason is deforestation. This is a process of a wide cleaning or cutting so many trees just for placing buildings or structures in that area and that just adds more pollution in our area. Our solution for that is to have a plant vegetation just like what the mind map has offered recently.

Starbursting Diagram

This is a Starbursting technique that aims to question and answer about a problem or solution using the questions. In this technique, the topic that we are focusing this time is all about one of our solutions that is plant vegetation. It is a solution that can help lessen the typhoon’s flooding impact.
